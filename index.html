    Starting the Race

    A good start is crucial! After the countdown, the "GO!" signal will appear.

    • Perfect Start: Press your key(s) right as "GO!" appears to get a starting boost and a special highlight for the race. The boost size can be changed in the advanced settings.
    • False Start: Pressing your key before "GO!" will cause you to stall for a moment after the race begins. Don't jump the gun! You can disable this penalty in the advanced settings.
    • Start Modes: You can choose between a classic single-button start or a more challenging two-button start in the advanced settings.

    Gameplay

    Once the race is on, press your assigned key(s) as quickly as you can to build up force. The more force you have, the faster your vehicle will move. But be careful, your force drains over time, so you need to keep tapping!
